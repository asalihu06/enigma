What is the Standard Tier?
Entry-level asset ownership
The Standard Tier is our accessible starting point — real asset ownership through a structured hire purchase plan. No guarantees beyond the contract. No opaque processes.
Tier A vs Tier B
Same destination. Different path.
Both tiers end in full ownership. Tier A has a higher deposit and shorter contract. Tier B has a lower deposit and a longer run. You choose based on what you can put down upfront.
What happens after?
Asset title transfers to you.
On your final weekly remittance, the asset becomes yours outright. No further obligations. Excess payments are credited and shorten your contract automatically.

The Process

From deposit
to ownership.

A clear, four-step path from your first enquiry to your first remittance — and eventually full ownership.

1

Choose Your Asset & Tier

Pick the asset class that matches your work, and select Tier A or Tier B based on your deposit capacity. Both end in full ownership.

2

Submit Your Request

Fill in the investment request form. Our team will contact you within 1–2 business days to verify eligibility and arrange the guarantor process.

3

Sign the Agreement & Pay Deposit

A formal written hire purchase agreement is issued and signed by both parties. Only after signing and receiving your deposit is the asset sourced and deployed.

4

Earn, Remit, Own

The asset is handed over and you begin operations. Weekly remittances reduce your outstanding balance until final payment — when ownership transfers to you completely.

Built-In Structure

Every plan includes these standards.

Not optional. Built into every Standard Tier agreement from day one.

Formal Written Agreement

Every plan is governed by a signed hire purchase contract covering the full schedule, deposit, remittance amounts, and ownership transfer. No verbal arrangements.

GPS Tracking on All Assets

Every vehicle is GPS-fitted before delivery. Bikes and cars include remote immobilization. Asset location is monitored continuously throughout the plan period.

Guarantor-Backed Contract

All hire purchase agreements above the Wheelbarrow tier require a guarantor. The guarantor is named in the agreement and is jointly responsible for the contract obligations.

Roadworthy Inspection

All vehicles pass a roadworthy inspection before delivery. Keke, motorbike, and automobile assets are verified for mechanical soundness and safe operation prior to handover.

Asset Insurance Included

Insurance is maintained on the asset throughout the plan period for Keke, motorbike, and automobile tiers. Cover is included as part of the plan — not an optional add-on.

Direct Team Access

Every plan comes with direct access to the Enigma team by phone and email. For automobile plans, a dedicated account officer is assigned to you for the contract duration.

Found your plan?

Submit a request and our team will confirm availability, walk you through the next steps, and issue your agreement — no pressure, no obligation at this stage.
