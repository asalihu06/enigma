Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

What is the difference between Tier A and Tier B?
Tier A requires a higher initial deposit and results in a shorter contract with a slightly lower total cost. Tier B has a lower deposit and a longer contract — paying slightly more overall. Both end in full legal ownership of the asset.
Do I need a bank account to apply?
Certain plan types may require a bank account for higher-value assets across all plan types.
What guarantor do I need?
One guarantor is required an employed professional, established business owner, or recognised community/religious leader. They sign a Deed of Indemnity covering the full contract value and assume liability in the event of default. This is a firm requirement with no exceptions.
What happens if I miss a weekly remittance?
One missed payment triggers an immediate follow-up. Two consecutive missed payments without resolution activates our recovery procedure: remote immobilization of the asset and formal escalation to the guarantor.
Can I pay off my plan early?
Yes and we encourage it. Any payment above the agreed weekly amount is applied directly to your outstanding balance, shortening your contract. There is no prepayment penalty.
Who handles maintenance during the plan?
Routine consumables like oil, filters, tyres, minor repairs are the driver's responsibility. Major structural failures (engine or gearbox) are covered by Enigma. All assets are fully serviced before delivery.
How long does delivery take after I pay the deposit?
Assets are sourced per confirmed client, not held in stock. For all other assets, we confirm a clear delivery timeline before you commit.
