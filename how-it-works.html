Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

What is the difference between Tier 1 and Tier 2?
Tier 1 (Standard) is designed for entry-level capital allocation into a single asset class, with a moderate performance-based return rate and monthly reporting. Tier 2 (Premium) is for larger capital deployment across multiple asset classes — it carries an enhanced return rate, priority weekly payout processing, and asset-level weekly reporting. Both tiers are governed by formal written agreements and operate under the same operational management framework.
Is my capital guaranteed?
No. All investments carry operational risk — returns are performance-based and not guaranteed. They reflect actual asset utilisation and can be affected by vehicle downtime, route conditions, and market variation. We disclose this clearly in every investor brief. We encourage all prospective investors to read the full risk disclosure before committing capital.
When and how are returns paid?
Returns are calculated from verified weekly asset revenue and paid on a fixed weekly settlement date. Each payout is accompanied by a performance summary. Premium investors receive enhanced weekly reports with asset-level data. There are no manual requests required — settlement is processed automatically each cycle.
What happens to my capital if an asset has downtime?
Downtime reduces the revenue generated by the asset during that period, which directly affects the return for that week. Enigma's operations team works to minimise downtime through proactive maintenance scheduling and rapid response. Downtime events are reported to investors in the performance summary accompanying each payout.
Is any payment collected on this website?
No. Capital is never collected through any public-facing page or online form. Funds are only received following a formal consultation, agreement execution, and manual verification by the Enigma investment team. If you encounter any request for payment that does not follow this process, do not proceed and contact us directly.
How do I access my investment performance data?
Currently, performance data is delivered via the weekly summary report issued alongside each payout. A dedicated investor dashboard providing real-time asset tracking and return monitoring is in active development — all active investors will be notified and migrated to the portal on launch.
Can I invest in multiple asset classes at the same time?
Yes — this is the defining characteristic of Tier 2 (Premium). Premium investors deploy capital across multiple asset classes simultaneously, with each asset class covered by the relevant section of the investment agreement. If you are on Tier 1 and wish to expand, contact the team to discuss an upgrade pathway.
