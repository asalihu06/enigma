By submitting a quote request, contacting us, or entering into a hire purchase agreement, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

Section 1

Overview

Enigma Transport Services ("Enigma", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who interact with us — including clients, guarantors, website visitors, and enquirers. This Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, store, use, and protect personal information in connection with our hire purchase services.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website, our quote request process, our hire purchase agreements, and any direct communication with our team via phone, email, or in person.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial or marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively to deliver, manage, and protect the hire purchase services you have requested or entered into with us.

Section 2

Data We Collect

We collect personal information in the following ways and from the following categories of individuals.

From Clients and Applicants

  • Full name, phone number, and email address.
  • Residential address and state of residence.
  • Occupation, employer name, or business description.
  • Details of the asset requested and preferred plan tier.
  • Payment records, including deposit and weekly remittance amounts, dates, and transfer evidence.
  • Identity information as may be required during the verification process (e.g. national ID, driver's licence).

From Guarantors

  • Full name, phone number, and email address.
  • Employment status, employer, or business details.
  • Residential address.
  • Signature on the Deed of Indemnity.

From Website Visitors and Enquirers

  • Name, phone number, and email address submitted via the contact or quote form.
  • Message content submitted via forms.
  • Basic website usage data (see Section 9 on Cookies).
Section 3

How We Use Your Data

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes only:

  • To process and assess your hire purchase application and determine eligibility.
  • To contact you by phone or email to discuss your quote, verify your identity, and walk you through the plan.
  • To prepare and execute your Hire Purchase Agreement.
  • To track your weekly remittances and maintain an accurate payment ledger.
  • To monitor and manage the asset under your hire purchase contract, including via GPS tracking (see Section 5).
  • To contact your Guarantor in the event of Default as set out in the Hire Purchase Agreement.
  • To respond to enquiries, complaints, or support requests.
  • To comply with our legal and contractual obligations.
  • To improve our services and processes internally.

We will not use your data for automated decision-making, profiling for marketing, or any purpose not directly related to the hire purchase services described above.

Section 4

Data Sharing

Enigma does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes. We only share personal data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Between client and guarantor: Relevant contract information is shared between the client and their nominated Guarantor as part of the hire purchase process. Both parties sign the same Hire Purchase Agreement and Deed of Indemnity.
  • With service providers: Where we engage trusted third-party service providers (e.g. GPS tracking providers, payment processors, or legal advisers), those providers receive only the data necessary to perform their service and are required to handle it confidentially.
  • With law enforcement or regulatory authorities: Where required by Nigerian law, a court order, or legitimate regulatory request, we may be required to disclose personal data. We will only do so to the minimum extent required.
  • In connection with asset recovery: In the event of Default, personal information may be shared with appropriate parties involved in the recovery process, including legal representatives.
Section 5

GPS & Location Data

All motorised assets deployed under active Hire Purchase Agreements are fitted with GPS tracking devices. By accepting delivery of the asset, the Client consents to the collection and use of GPS location data throughout the contract period.

What We Collect

  • Real-time and historical location of the asset.
  • Movement history, operational status, and engine activity of the asset.

How We Use GPS Data

  • To verify that the asset is being operated within agreed parameters.
  • To support asset recovery in the event of Default or theft.
  • To activate remote engine immobilization where permitted under the Hire Purchase Agreement.

Limits on GPS Data Use

GPS location data is used solely for asset management and contract enforcement. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose outside of the hire purchase arrangement. GPS data is retained for the duration of the active contract and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.

Section 6

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, and to meet our legal and contractual obligations.

  • Active clients: Personal and financial data is retained for the full duration of the hire purchase contract and for a minimum of five years following contract completion or termination, in accordance with standard business record-keeping requirements.
  • Guarantors: Guarantor data is retained for the same period as the related client's contract.
  • Unsuccessful applicants and enquirers: Data submitted through quote or contact forms where no agreement was entered into is retained for up to 12 months, after which it is securely deleted.
  • Payment records: All remittance records are retained for a minimum of five years following the end of the relevant contract for financial compliance purposes.

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

Section 7

Your Rights

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data held by Enigma Transport Services:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to correction: If any of your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request that we correct it.
  • Right to deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data. This right is subject to our legal and contractual obligations — we cannot delete data that we are required to retain under the terms of an active or recently completed hire purchase agreement.
  • Right to object: You may object to the use of your personal data for any purpose that is not strictly necessary for the performance of your hire purchase contract.
  • Right to data portability: You may request that we provide your personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable Nigerian law.

Section 8

Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and have implemented reasonable organisational and technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

  • Client files and contract records are stored securely and accessible only to authorised Enigma team members on a need-to-know basis.
  • Payment records and personal data transmitted electronically are handled through secure channels.
  • GPS data is stored and accessed through secured systems provided by our GPS tracking partner.

No system is completely immune to security risks. In the event of a data breach that is likely to affect your rights or interests, we will notify you and any relevant authority as required by applicable law.

Section 9

Website & Cookies

Our website may collect basic usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with the site. This may include pages visited, time spent on the site, device type, and referral source. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to individual identities unless you have submitted a form.

Cookies

Our website may use standard browser cookies to support site functionality and improve user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We do not use cookies to track your behaviour across other websites or to serve targeted advertising.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality but will not prevent you from accessing any of our core content or forms.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a minor, we will take steps to delete that data promptly.

If you believe we may have collected information from a minor, please contact us using the details in Section 12.

Section 11

Changes to This Policy

Enigma Transport Services may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or applicable law. When we make changes, the updated policy will be published on this page with a revised date at the top.

Where changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify active clients. Continued use of our services following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy for any new data collected after the revision date.

Changes to this Privacy Policy do not retroactively alter the rights of individuals whose data was collected under a prior version of the policy, except where required by law.
