Risk & Transparency

What Every Investor Should Understand

Investment returns are tied to real operational performance and are influenced by external market and operational conditions.

Performance-Based Returns

Returns are directly tied to each asset's real operational output. They are not fixed or guaranteed — they reflect actual vehicle utilisation and route performance on a weekly basis.

Operational Risk

As with any asset-backed structure, investments carry operational risk — including vehicle downtime, route demand variation, fuel costs, and general market conditions in the transport sector.

Controlled Operations

All risk mitigation at the operational level is managed exclusively by Enigma. Investors have no operational role or liability — risk management is built into the platform's infrastructure.

Fully Documented

Every investment is formalised through a written agreement specifying asset class, capital amount, return parameters, payout schedule, and termination conditions before any funds are received.

No Public Payment Flow

Capital is never collected through public-facing pages. Funds are only received after a formal agreement is executed and manual verification is completed by our investment team.

Ongoing Oversight

Fleet performance, maintenance schedules, and revenue collection are monitored continuously by Enigma's operations team, with regular reporting issued to all active investors.

Important Disclosure

All investment returns are performance-based and not guaranteed
